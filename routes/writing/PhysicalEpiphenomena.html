Megan McGee.

Physical Epiphenomena

The stillness of your body, let other bodies determine the limits of your personal space,

sardine packed metal box of a subway.

The angles and degrees that a body learns to bend.

You are met with the number of unmade friend; merciless confrontation.

Unmade friends like your threadbare twin sheets, that cling to your bed posts daily, static electricity and dry air of your prewar closet space.

We move into closets and out of closets when we come to “the city”

This town is for those of us who have a self to do battle with

Post-precipitated. cesspools of major cross streets,

Gravitational pull, the only higher power. It presses your buttons and presses your torso to the pavement with a chuckle

Gravity seated to the left of Luck at this judges table. To the right, Cash.

The grand prize goes to the tallest, richest, luckiest sonofabitch on this block!
